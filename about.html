Hello.

I'm Rashibul Islam — an optometrist from Murshidabad, West Bengal. I graduated from NSHM Knowledge Campus with a B.Optom degree and have since worked across hospital and retail settings, gaining a broad picture of what eye care looks like across different environments.

I started Optoverse because I kept noticing the same thing in practice: patients had questions their appointments never had time to answer. So I decided to write it all down — clearly, honestly, without the jargon.

My Journey

Education
B.Optom — NSHM Knowledge Campus
Built my foundation in ocular anatomy, refraction, binocular vision, contact lens practice, and low vision rehabilitation.
Clinical
50-Day Rural Eye Camp — Diamond Harbour
One of the most defining experiences of my career. Personally examined over a thousand patients across multiple rural locations with Sebaashray — many of whom had never had an eye exam in their lives. Observed high cataract prevalence and an unusually low rate of astigmatism in the population.
Hospital
Optometrist — Netradeep Eye Hospital
Gained hands-on hospital-based clinical experience — comprehensive eye examinations, patient counselling, and working alongside ophthalmologists in a structured clinical environment.
Retail & Community
Optometrist — Checkoro.com Opticals, Kolkata
Broadened my experience in optical retail — prescriptions, dispensing, contact lens fitting, and patient education for a diverse urban patient base.
The Goal
My Own Clinic — Murshidabad
Everything I've done has been building toward one thing: a small, quality eye clinic back home in Murshidabad — where access to proper eye care is still limited and where it matters the most.

What Optoverse Is

Optoverse is my attempt to bridge the gap between what optometrists know and what patients actually understand. Every article here is written the way I'd explain it to someone sitting across from me — no unnecessary jargon, no exaggeration, just honest information.

The notes section is for optometry students — the kind of concise, exam-focused material I wished existed when I was studying.
