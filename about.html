Hello.

I'm Rashibul — an optometrist based in Kolkata, originally from Murshidabad. I started Optoverse because I kept noticing the same thing in my practice: people had questions their doctors never answered. Not because the doctors were bad — just because there's never enough time in a consultation to explain everything.

So I decided to write it down.

My Background

One of the most formative experiences of my career. Personally examined over a thousand patients across multiple rural locations with Sebaashray — many of whom had never had an eye exam before. Observed high cataract prevalence and unusually low rates of astigmatism in the population.

Why Optoverse?

Most eye health content online is either too clinical (written for doctors) or too vague (written by people who aren't). I wanted something in the middle — accurate, honest, and written in the kind of language I actually use with patients.

Optoverse is also a resource for optometry students. The notes section contains study materials, solved past papers, and clinical summaries — things I wished existed when I was studying.
